Welcome to Davao del Sur: From Highland to Ocean

Discover the unparalleled beauty of Davao del Sur, a province where adventure and culture naturally intertwine. Located in the southeastern part of Mindanao, this province is a haven for eco-tourists, thrill-seekers, and cultural enthusiasts alike. Whether you are looking to conquer the towering heights of Mt. Apo, relax on the pristine white sands of our coastal municipalities, or immerse yourself in the rich heritage of the Bagobo and B'laan tribes, Davao del Sur offers a journey like no other.
Explore our scenic highlands, dive into our vibrant marine sanctuaries, and taste the freshest tropical fruits right from the source. Your ultimate Mindanao adventure starts right here.

Thrilling Adventures

Conquer epic mountain trails, navigate river rapids, and experience world-class outdoor thrills.

Coastal Wonders

Discover hidden coves, vibrant marine sanctuaries, and tranquil shores along the Davao Gulf.

Rich Agriculture

Taste the region's sweetest tropical fruits and sip premium, locally-grown highland coffee.

Discover Davao del Sur

Davao del Sur is a province in the southeastern part of Mindanao, blessed with remarkable natural wonders and a vibrant cultural heritage. From the towering summit of Mt. Apo to the serene shores of Malalag Bay, this province offers a diverse range of experiences for every type of traveler.

Travel Tips

Getting There

Fly to Francisco Bangoy International Airport in Davao City, then take a bus south.

Best Time to Visit

November to May is the dry season — ideal for outdoor activities and beach trips.

Budget Friendly

Davao del Sur offers affordable accommodations, food, and transport options.
